Squirrel

The African pygmy squirrel is the smallest squirrel in the world, being a mere 70 millimeters in length

  • Scientific Name: Sciuridae
  • Average Length: 7-10 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 5-10 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Rainforest

Squirrels are members of the family Sciuridae, a family that includes small or medium-size rodents. The squirrel family includes tree squirrels, ground squirrels, chipmunks and marmots amongst other rodents.
